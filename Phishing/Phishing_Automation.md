GoPhish \- (Open-Source Phishing Framework) \- [getgophish.com](http://getgophish.com)

- Web-based framework to make setting up phishing campaigns more straightforward  
- Store your SMTP server settings for sending emails, has a web-based tool for creating email templates  
- Can schedule when emails are sent  
- Has an analytics dashboard that shows how many emails have been sent, opened or clicked

SET \- (Social Engineering Toolkit) \- [trustedsec.com](http://trustedsec.com)

- Multitude of tools  
- Ability to create spear-phishing attacks and deploy fake versions of common websites to trick victims into entering their credentials
