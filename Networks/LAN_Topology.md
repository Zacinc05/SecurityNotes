Local Area Network (LAN) Topologies

- Star  
  - Devices are individually connected via a central networking device  
  - Reliable and Scalable, but costly  
- Bus  
  - Backbone cable  
    - Relies upon a single connection  
  - Prone to slowness/bottleneck  
  - Hard to investigate issues  
  - Easier and cost effective  
- Ring  
  - Sending data across the loop until it reaches the destined device  
  - Single directional  
  - Relies on all devices operating  
  - Easy to troubleshoot  
  - Not efficient
