Incident Response Techniques

- SIEM (Security Information and Event Management Solution)  
  - Collects logs to centralized location  
  - Correlates them to identify incidents  
- AV (Antivirus)  
  - Detects known malicious programs  
  - Regular scans of system  
- EDR (Endpoint Detection & Response)  
  - Deploy on systems to protect against threats
